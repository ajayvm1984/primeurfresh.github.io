What Sets Us Apart

A standard, not a catalogue.

We are not a market stall and not a retailer. We are the house that great chefs rely on — a single, accountable partner for produce that arrives correct, consistent, and on time, every service.

Peak-Season Selection

Every line is chosen at its prime — graded by hand, never by volume. If it isn't fit for a Michelin pass, it doesn't leave our floor.
